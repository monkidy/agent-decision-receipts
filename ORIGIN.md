# Origin

This work starts from a practical trust question:

When software starts acting on behalf of people, how do we keep responsibility visible?

The entry point is not only technical capability.
It is trust, proof, permission, and what a human can responsibly stand behind.

This notebook comes from a business and relationship perspective:
delegated action only works when the surrounding system can explain itself afterward.

## What this origin is not

- Not a personal diary
- Not a founder manifesto
- Not a claim of inevitability or superiority
- Not an export of any private operating system

## Working sentence

Show the voice.
Do not expose private life.
