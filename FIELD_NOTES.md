# Field Notes

Short notes from the bench. Professional, direct, proof-oriented.

---

A refusal is not a failure if it prevents an unauthorized action.

---

A draft is not a permission to publish.

---

A smarter agent does not solve an authority problem.

---

If an action cannot be explained afterward, it should not be trusted before.

---

Receipts do not make agents safe. They make actions inspectable.

---

No action without a receipt.
No authority without boundaries.
No trust without proof.
