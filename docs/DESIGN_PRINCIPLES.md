# Design Principles

Creative discipline for a living proof notebook.

## Core principles

Structure without coldness.
Sincerity without personal leakage.
Originality without theatre.
Proof without corporate bullshit.
Boundaries without killing the living voice.

## BCA route in public-safe language

**B - Structure for proof**
Receipts, schema, examples, validation, and clear status.

**C - Atmosphere for human memory**
Field notes, plain language, and a notebook feeling.

**A - Restraint for trust**
No hype, no overclaim, no fake authority, no private export.

## Operating formula

Use structure for proof.
Use atmosphere for sincerity.
Use restraint for trust.

## Target feeling

A clean field notebook.
A serious archive page.
A proof table.
A working bench.

Not a glossy pitch deck.
Not a cyber dashboard.
Not a compliance theatre set.
